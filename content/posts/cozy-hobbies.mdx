# Finding Joy in Cozy Hobbies

I think there's a special kind of magic in hobbies that don't have a purpose beyond making you happy. No side hustle, no monetization strategy — just doing something because it fills your cup.

## Watercolor Painting

I started with one of those beginner kits from the craft store and honestly? The results are charmingly terrible and I love every single one. There's something so meditative about watching colors bleed into each other on wet paper.

## Journaling & Scrapbooking

My journal is a chaotic mix of daily reflections, pasted-in ticket stubs, polaroid photos, washi tape borders, and random doodles. It's not aesthetic in the Pinterest way, but flipping through it makes me so happy.

## Reading Before Bed

I made a rule: no phone in bed, only books. It took about a week to stop reaching for my phone, but now I look forward to that quiet reading time. Currently working through a stack of novels that's taller than my nightstand.

## Baking Experiments

Saturday mornings are for baking now. Last week it was cinnamon rolls (from scratch!), this week I'm attempting macarons. The kitchen is a mess but the house smells incredible.

## The Takeaway

You don't have to be good at something to enjoy it. That's the whole point.
