# Favorite Moments
- First time sledding - down a golf course at that
- Playing Ticket to Ride
- Cheffin it up - making pasta
- Chilly night hot tubbing and Moscatto drinking

## Day 1

Our very first solo road trip!!! We set off on our nearly 3 hour journey to Galena. On the way there, we never once played music; just yapped the day away. :3
About 2 hours into the trip, we decided to make a pit stop for some Mcchickens and for the Hobby Town in Rockford. It turned out to be a fun little pit stop as we even found the cutest little beaver puppet!
Then back on the road for the last hour. We were amazed by the hilly views. Once we got to the AirBnB, we were pleasantly surprised by the small but adorable place. We checked it out then headed to the town for some dinner at the Galena tRaphouse. We then walked it off around town just looking at the (closed) shops then headed back to our new home.
Stranger Things filled the time before hopping into the hot tub. Although it was very dark and a little scary, we still managed to have a good time with some wine.

## Day 2

The first full day of our trip was a jam packed adventure like no other. We started off the day by making delicious breakfast consisting of egg and chorizo on bread. Of course, the master chef brought his spices from home to add to our meal.
Afterwards, we set off an expedition to find a sled. We drove to a few Galena stores but finally had some luck in Dubuque, lowa! Unsurprisingly, finding hills we could ride down was the next challenging task. We had some fun on the baby hills and feeling the icy chill on our cheeks. However, it wasn't until we trekked the snowy plains (crossed the street) that we realized the golf course would prove a great ally. This course was awesome we nearly got airtime riding down... We'd also get a killer splash from the snow. But we didn't get our fill. We drove and found one more hill to dive down and it nearly took Kevin out...
We then headed back home to shower, warm up, and make a tasty pasta dinner. Although while we were making dinner, we realized an alarm was going off, likely testing the tornado siren...
It was still early so we decided to take a little car ride to lowa for some late night sightseeing. When we got back, played Ticket to Ride and every time someone put a route down, the other player had a consequence. By the end of the night, I was spooked...
