# My Current Fashion Favorites

There's something about the transition between winter and spring that makes me want to completely overhaul my wardrobe. Not in a dramatic, throw-everything-out way, but more of a gentle reshuffling — pulling out pieces I forgot I loved and pairing them with new finds.

## The Oversized Blazer

I finally invested in a really good oversized blazer in a warm camel color, and it has genuinely changed my daily outfit formula. Throw it over a simple white tee and jeans? Instantly polished. Over a slip dress for dinner? Chef's kiss.

## Gold Jewelry Layering

I've been layering delicate gold necklaces like it's my job. Three thin chains of varying lengths, a couple of rings, and small hoop earrings. The key is keeping each piece minimal so they all work together without competing.

## The Perfect Tote

Found the most beautiful structured tote in a deep burgundy leather. It fits my laptop, a book, my water bottle, and still looks sleek. It's become my everyday bag and I get compliments on it constantly.

## What I'm Eyeing Next

A pair of ballet flats in a fun color — maybe cherry red or sage green. And definitely more linen pieces for when it actually warms up. Spring, I'm ready for you!
