## Day 1

## Day 2

I've been layering delicate gold necklaces like it's my job. Three thin chains of varying lengths, a couple of rings, and small hoop earrings. The key is keeping each piece minimal so they all work together without competing.

## Day 3

Found the most beautiful structured tote in a deep burgundy leather. It fits my laptop, a book, my water bottle, and still looks sleek. It's become my everyday bag and I get compliments on it constantly.
