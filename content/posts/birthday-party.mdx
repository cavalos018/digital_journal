# The Most Magical Birthday Party

I've never been the type to make a big deal out of my birthday, but this year my friends had other plans. And honestly? I'm so glad they did.

## The Surprise

They told me we were going to a casual dinner. What I walked into was a room full of fairy lights, balloons in the prettiest shades of blush and gold, and every single person I love singing (off-key, beautifully) "Happy Birthday."

I definitely cried. The good kind of crying.

## The Details

The cake was a work of art — three tiers of vanilla bean with raspberry filling and the most delicate buttercream flowers on top. Almost too pretty to eat. Almost.

There were sparklers (indoor ones, don't worry), a photo booth with silly props, and a playlist that was basically the soundtrack of our friendship.

## The Feeling

What got me the most wasn't the decorations or the cake — it was realizing how loved I am. In the busyness of everyday life, you sometimes forget. But standing in that room, looking at all those faces, I felt it so deeply.

## A New Year, A New Chapter

Another trip around the sun, and I'm going into this year feeling grateful, excited, and ready for whatever comes next. Here's to the next chapter.
